About Ouroboros

Ouroboros is a self-contained workspace for thinkers, builders, and creators. Every tool here lives in your browser — no accounts, no installs, no cloud dependency.

Named after the ancient serpent that consumes itself to be reborn, Ouroboros embodies the philosophy that every ending fuels a new beginning.
